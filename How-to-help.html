If you know a child who is being abused, or are one yourself, get help IMMEDIATELY. Call the child abuse hotline 1(800)-422-4453, or 1-800-4-A-CHILD, or simply 911. Never stay silent

Symtoms:

  • Tendancies to avoid relatives, people, friends, or other children.
  • Bad grades or low school motivation.
  • Easy irritaion and agression.
  • Emotionally unstable, for example crying often, anxiety, or panic attacks.
  • Clinging or sticking close to a certain person.
  • Often physical sicknesses.
  • Self-Harm, or hurting others.
  • More risky, or dangerous actions.
  • Malnourishment
