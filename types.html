Fact: Abuse hurts children every day

Five children die of abuse every day. This is five children too many, and this fact does not even encompass all of the children who are hurt untill they want to die, but still hang on. There are also still the kids who are not able to learn in school because of psychiatric disorders like depression and anxiety that happened because of constant abuse.

In addition to this, there are children who are sexualy, physically, and emotionally torn down until they feel like they are nothing. The fact is that this is wrong.

But we can do something to help these children. If you know someone who is abused call the child abuse hotline number. Tell somebody, so the victim can get help. And maybe, you will save a life.

Physical Abuse

  • Kicking, biting, scratching, burning, strangling, or punching a victim.
  • Throwing objects at the victim.
  • Pulling a victim's clothing or hair.
  • Grabbing a victim without permission.
  • Forcing a victim to go places or restricting the victim's movement.

Emotional and Verbal Abuse

  • Calling the victim names.
  • Yelling or screaming
  • intentionally embarrasing victim in public
  • preventing victim from seeing or talking to friends or family
  • telling you what to do or wear
  • damaging victim's property
  • using profound language
  • using online places to control, humiliate, or intimidate
  • Blaming victim's actions for their abusive behavior

Sexual Abuse

  • Unwanted kissing or touching
  • Unwanted rough or violent sexual activity
  • Rape or attempted rape
  • Threatening someone into unwanted sexual activity
  • Using sexual insults toward someone
  • child pornography

Child Neglect

  • a history of a child being gived inadequit care
  • poor hygiene
  • little or no weight gain
  • little to no medical care
  • frequently absent from school
  • child pornography
